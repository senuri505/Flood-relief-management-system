Flood Relief Management System

During flood situations, many families struggle to access basic needs. This system allows affected households to quickly request food, water, medicine, or shelter and stay connected with relief support.

About This System

The Flood Relief Management System is designed to assist households affected by floods by enabling them to request essential support such as food, water, medicine, and temporary shelter. Users can submit their needs quickly, monitor the status of their requests, and ensure that relief reaches them efficiently. The system improves coordination between affected communities and relief providers, helping aid reach those in urgent need.

Available Relief Services

Food

Dry rations and cooked meals provided to flood-affected families to meet basic nutritional needs.

Water

Safe drinking water and sanitation support to prevent waterborne diseases in affected areas.

Medicine

Essential medical supplies and health support to maintain well-being during emergencies.

Shelter

Temporary accommodation for displaced families, ensuring safety and protection during floods.

Flood Severity Levels

  • Low - Minor water level, manageable situation
  • Medium - Partial house damage or limited access
  • High - Severe flooding, urgent assistance required

How to Request Relief

  1. Login or Sign Up to access your account
  2. Fill out and submit a new relief request
  3. View all your submitted requests
  4. Edit or update any request if needed
  5. Delete a request if it is no longer required
  6. Logout when you are done

Important Instructions

  • Provide accurate location
  • Enter valid contact number
  • Mention urgent needs clearly
  • Track request status
